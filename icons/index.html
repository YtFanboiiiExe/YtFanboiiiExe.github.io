Exe's Icon Creator
A python script (compiled to exe) that allows you to get custom icons (circle icos, hexagon icons, and etc)
Import a blank icon and a jailbreak theme of your choice (or the original icons) and choose your shape and apply the theme

Get Exe's Icon themer (unavailable) Get cowabunga lite (required for applying themes) Join Discord Server
HOW TO USE:
Step 1: Get the blank icons and send them to your pc
Step 2: Get cowabunga lite And exe's icon themer
Step 3:Download the jailbreak theme that you wanna apply
(optinal) Sort the icons (according to whats on your homescreen kinda like what I did here:

Step 4:Import blank icon, import the folder with the icons, and select the shape you want
Step 5: Open the folders with the icons that you applied and go to the folder (in this case: 1) and copy the icons to another folder on the desktop
Step 6: Turn off find my iphone on the phone you want to apply the icons to
Step 7: Open cowabunga lite, plug your iphoe into your pc and trust it (MAKE SURE ITUNES IS INSTALLED AND NO FROM THE MICROSOFT STORE), Click icon theming and import the folder with the applied icons
Step 8:Once finished, Go through the setup and you will see the icons on the homescreen, Adjust them to fit your wallpaper and turn find my iphone back on and your done!
